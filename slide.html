Gameplay, Physics, & Systems Pitch

Gameplay Overview

  • REQ Local Multiplayer: Two-player head-to-head combat sharing a single keyboard .
  • BONUS Independent Turret: Tank body and turret rotation are controlled completely independently .
  • Pacing: 60 FPS action in a 960 × 640 window .

Winning the Match

  • Round Victory: Win a round by destroying the enemy tank OR collecting 30 food points .
  • Match Victory: The first player to reach 5 round wins claims the match .
  • Persistence: Health (3 HP) and round scores reset each round, but uncollected food remains on the map .

Map System Architecture

  • Maps are defined as 24 × 16 grids of text characters .
  • The MapRegistry automatically loads maps at import time .
  • Four built-in arenas: Classic, Fortress, Arena, and Open Field .

Grid Parsing & Generation

REQUIREMENT: Array/Grid Map Data
  • build_walls() parses the grid into physical objects .
  • # becomes indestructible Steel walls .
  • B becomes destructible Brick walls .
  • . becomes open tiles mapped as valid item spawn coordinates via get_open_tiles() .

Destructible Environments

BONUS: Destructible Arena
  • Hit Points: Brick walls start with 2 HP (`BRICK_HEALTH = 2`) .
  • Visual States: The visual_state property updates immediately upon taking damage .
  • Destruction: At 0 HP, walls transition to DESTROYED and are removed from physics and rendering calculations .

Physics: Tank Kinematics

REQUIREMENT: Vector Movement & Trigonometry
  • Frame-Rate Independence: All movement scales by delta-time (`dt`) .
  • Base Speeds: Tanks move at 120 px/s, rotate bodies at 180 deg/s, and rotate turrets at 200 deg/s .
  • Vector Math: Movement direction is derived dynamically from body rotation: velocity.y = speed * sin(θ) .

Collision: Axis-Split Resolution

REQUIREMENT: Tank vs. Wall Collision (AABB)
  • The CollisionManager resolves wall overlapping independently on the X and Y axes .
  • Benefit: Instead of sticking to walls, tanks smoothly slide along flat surfaces and corners .
  • Destroyed walls are explicitly ignored during the _blocked() checks .

Collision: Boundary Containment

  • Tanks are restricted strictly to the playable area .
  • The clamp() function locks coordinates between TANK_SIZE / 2 (20px) and the screen edges (940px / 620px) .
  • Screen edge containment is evaluated before wall collision, ensuring unbreakable boundaries .

Collision: Implementation

def clamp(value: float, low: float, high: float) -> float:
    return max(low, min(high, value))

def _blocked(rect: pygame.Rect) -> bool:
    if any(wall.rect.colliderect(rect) for wall in walls if not wall.is_destroyed):
        return True
    if other_tank_rect is not None and other_tank_rect.colliderect(rect):
        return True
    return False

Sliding: Implementation

test_x = pygame.Vector2(tank_position.x, previous_position.y)
test_x.x = clamp(test_x.x, half, SCREEN_WIDTH - half)
if _blocked(_tank_rect(test_x)):
    test_x.x = previous_position.x
test_y = pygame.Vector2(test_x.x, tank_position.y)
test_y.y = clamp(test_y.y, half, SCREEN_HEIGHT - half)
if _blocked(_tank_rect(test_y)):
    test_y.y = previous_position.y
return test_y

Physics: Bullet Projectiles

  • Bullets travel at 280 px/s .
  • REQ Limits: Max 5 live bullets simultaneously per player, with cooldown .
  • Expiry: Bullets vanish after 5 wall bounces OR after 10 seconds of airtime .
  • REQ Hit Logic: Bullet vs. Enemy Tank collision results in damage .

Physics: Wall Reflection

REQUIREMENT: Vector Reflection Physics
  • When a bullet overlaps a wall, _collision_normal() calculates the shallowest penetration depth to determine the impact face (X or Y) .
  • reflect_velocity() mirrors the bullet's vector across that normal .
  • The bullet is physically pushed out of the wall by the overlap distance + 0.1px to prevent getting stuck .

Physics: Reflection

def reflect_velocity(velocity: pygame.Vector2, normal: pygame.Vector2) -> pygame.Vector2:
    # Reflection formula: v' = v - 2 * (v dot n) * n
    return velocity - 2 * velocity.dot(normal) * normal

Power-Up Subsystem

BONUS: Power-ups
  • Managed entirely by the PowerUpManager .
  • Spawning: Drops a random power-up every 8.0s onto an open tile, capped at 2 simultaneous items on the map .
  • State: Timers are transferred directly into the Tank's active state dictionary upon collision .

Power-Ups: Speed & Shield

  • Speed Boost: Multiplies base tank speed by 1.5x for 5.0 seconds. Excellent for flanking and objective rushing .
  • Shield: Completely absorbs the next bullet impact. Lasts up to 8.0 seconds and is visually indicated by an alpha-blended glow .

Power-Ups: Triple Shot

  • Lasts for 6.0 seconds .
  • Overrides standard firing to spawn three bullets simultaneously .
  • Side bullets are offset mathematically by ±15.0 degrees (`TRIPLE_SHOT_SPREAD`) from the central turret angle .
